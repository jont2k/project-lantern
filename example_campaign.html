Key Issues

Our campaign is focused on addressing the issues that matter most to our community.

Healthcare For All

We're fighting for a healthcare system that works for everyone, not just the wealthy and well-connected. Our plan ensures quality, affordable healthcare access for every family in our district.

Education Investment

Our children deserve world-class schools with well-paid teachers and modern resources. We're committed to investing in education from pre-K through college and vocational training.

Environmental Protection

Climate change demands bold action. Our comprehensive plan will create green jobs while protecting our natural resources for future generations.

Economic Opportunity

We're working to build an economy that works for everyone, with family-sustaining jobs, support for small businesses, and investments in our infrastructure.

Community Safety

Every neighborhood deserves to be safe. We support common-sense reforms and community-based solutions that build trust and protect everyone.

Affordable Housing

Everyone deserves a safe, affordable place to call home. Our housing policy will address skyrocketing costs and expand access to quality housing for all.
