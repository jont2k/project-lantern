Our Menu

Whether you're starting your morning with a latte, craving a mid-day pick-me-up, or looking to unwind with a cozy pastry—Lantern Café has something brewed just for you.

Coffee & Espresso

  • Espresso – Rich and bold, served straight or with a splash of milk.
  • Americano – Smooth espresso with hot water for a clean finish.
  • Cappuccino – Espresso with steamed milk and a foamy top.
  • Latte – Creamy and comforting with optional flavors (vanilla, caramel, mocha).
  • Cold Brew – Slow-steeped and served over ice, strong yet smooth.

Teas & Refreshers

  • Chai Latte – Spiced black tea with steamed milk and honey.
  • Herbal Tea – A selection of calming blends: mint, chamomile, and hibiscus.
  • Iced Hibiscus Lemonade – Tart and floral, perfect for a sunny day.

Bakery & Bites

  • Butter Croissant – Flaky, golden, and fresh out of the oven.
  • Coffee Cake – Cinnamon-swirled and crumb-topped, pairs perfectly with your brew.
  • Bagel with Cream Cheese – Toasted to your liking, simple and satisfying.
  • Avocado Toast – Sourdough, smashed avocado, red chili flakes, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  • Breakfast Sandwich – Egg, cheese, and your choice of bacon or sausage on a fresh roll.

All items available Monday–Friday from 7 AM to 1 PM
